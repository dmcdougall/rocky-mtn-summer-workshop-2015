    Workshop Venue

    The workshop will be held in the Student Commons Building at The University of Colorado Denver, part of the Auraria Campus in Downtown Denver. A map of the campus, including the Auraria West Station, the Campus Village, the Student Commons Building, and a walking route from the Campus Village to the Student Commons Building can be found here.

    Travel

    Flights should be booked into Denver International Airport. Transportation options between DIA and the Auraria Campus include public buses (RTD), shared-ride shuttles, and taxis. Uber and Lyft also operate in the Denver area. The cheapest option is to take the RTD shuttle. RTD buses pick up and drop-off from Jeppesen Terminal Level 5, island 5, outside doors 506 and 510. Take the AF (Downtown Denver) bus to Union Station (~45 minutes). From Union Station, you can walk (~30 minutes) to the Auraria Campus or take the light rail to the Auraria West Station, which is next to the Campus Village (outlined in red here here). Several lines take this route.

    Accommodations

    Those receiving travel support have been booked accommodations at the Campus Village, located on the Auraria Campus next to the Auraria West Station, for July 14-18. The booking includes linens and towels, as well as breakfast each morning.

    Those not receiving travel support must arrange their own accommodations. There are many hotels in Downtwon Denver near the Auraria Campus. The SpringHill Suites Marriot in Downtown Denver is on the Auraria campus right next to the Student Commons Building.

    Dining

    For those staying at the Campus Village, breakfast is provided each morning. On Wednesday and Thursday, lunch will be provided for all workshop participants at the workshop venue. For lunch on Friday and all dinners, there are many great dining options in the area.
